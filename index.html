Ben Muster

Ben Muster is a writer who grew up in regional Victoria. His stories scrutinise the collisions of Australian masculinity and queerness. Currently completing his Bachelor of Arts (Creative Writing) at RMIT University, his recent writing includes his ongoing short story anthology 'Mutuals', and his work-in-progress novel 'Turnoff'.
